## Session Archive

This archive contains all recorded sessions published as part of **Dead Air Project**.

Each session includes:
- documented location and method
- raw and (when available) enhanced audio
- timestamped EVP markers
- confidence-based observations

Sessions are presented chronologically.  
Use individual session pages to listen, analyze, and interpret recordings in detail.
